# Birthday Website Rebuild Plan

## SPRINT 1: Emotional Content & Story Overhaul
- Hero: Personal emotional hook — "To the boy who became my hero"
- About: Real story with childhood memories, shared moments, inside jokes
- Timeline: Emotional milestones — first bike, graduation, fights & makeups, him standing by you
- Gallery: Story-driven captions (each photo tells a chapter)
- Letters Section: 4-5 message cards from different family voices (Mom, Dad, sibling, best friend)
- Special Message: A tear-jerking heartfelt letter — specific, not generic
- "25 Reasons" section: Why he's amazing, one for each year
- Future Wishes: Your dreams for his next 25 years

## SPRINT 2: Glassmorphism + Skeuomorphism Design System
- Thick frosted glass: backdrop-filter: blur(24px), rgba(20,20,30,0.4), 1px border rgba(255,255,255,0.1)
- Skeuomorphic depth: Multi-layered realistic shadows, inner bevel highlights, 3D push buttons
- Glow accents: Soft golden ambient glow behind cards, hover glow expansion
- Custom ornamental dividers, hand-drawn style flourishes
- Color: Midnight navy (#070710) + Champagne gold (#d4af37) + Warm cream (#faf6f0)

## SPRINT 3: Layout, Cards & Readability
- Every card MUST pop from background (strong borders, padding, shadow depth)
- Glass cards on dark textured backgrounds
- Proper contrast ratios for all text
- Improved spacing rhythm (80px-120px sections)
- Responsive grid that breathes

## SPRINT 4: Custom Effects & Polish
- 3D tilt cards on mouse move
- Golden sparkle cursor trail
- Ambient floating golden dust particles
- Typewriter text reveal for headings
- Smooth scroll-triggered entrance animations
- Loading screen with golden particle burst

## SPRINT 5: Testing & Audit
- All links, images, buttons verified
- Mobile responsiveness check
- Emotional content read-through
- Design consistency audit
- Performance check
